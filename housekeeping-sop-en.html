Housekeeping SOP (B&B Rooms)

✓ 0/22 tasks ⏰ Ready by 4 PM
⏰ Deadline: All rooms must be ready by 4:00 PM. Guests check out by 10:00 AM. Check Live Rota for room assignments. Leave key in the lock when finished.
🛏️ Step 1
Beds & linens
▼
Remove used sheets and pillowcases from bed
Put dirty linens in the laundry basket
Get fresh linen from cupboard (up brown stairs, first door right)
Make bed with fresh sheets and pillowcases
Place one big towel + one small towel per person on top of cover
Ensure extra blankets are neatly folded at foot of bed
🗄️ Linen Location: Up brown stairs, first door to the right. Big towels (wardrobe right), small towels (wardrobe left), bath mats & tablecloths (lower left). Always refill cupboards after taking items.
🧹 Step 2
Room cleaning
▼
Colour-coded cleaning system
  • ●Green: General surfaces (furniture, windowsills, tables)
  • ●Blue: Glass, mirrors, windows
  • ●Pink: Toilets only
Hoover entire bedroom floor thoroughly
Dust all surfaces with green cloth (furniture, windowsills, shelves)
Clear table of dishes → take to breakfast kitchen and clean
Set up table for new guests: cups, saucers, teaspoons, glasses, serviettes
Empty bin if full, replace with new bin bag
Open window briefly to air out the room
🧽 Cleaning supplies: Multi-purpose cleaner, glass cleaner, air freshener, Viakal disinfectant, toilet cleaner. All stored in upstairs cleaning cupboard (up brown stairs, turn left, first door).
🚿 Step 3
Bathroom deep clean
▼
Clean all surfaces with green cloth (sink, counters, shelves)
Clean mirrors and windows with blue cloth
Clean toilet thoroughly with pink cloth and toilet cleaner
Check floor for hair/dust, wipe down if needed
⚠️ Never cross-contaminate: Pink cloth is ONLY for toilets. Green for general surfaces. Blue for glass/mirrors. Store them separately after cleaning.
🧴 Step 4
Amenities & final checks
▼
Refill hand soap if half empty (refillers in cleaning cupboard/storage)
Check body wash/conditioner levels, refill if needed
Replace toilet roll if halfway used (put used one in staff bathrooms)
Place fresh bath mat on floor
Restock tissues if box is low
Double-check: bed made, towels placed, bathroom floor clean
Leave room key in the lock
Return all cleaning tools to cupboard
✅ Quick final checklist: Bed? Towels? Table set? Bathroom? Bin? Key in lock? If yes to all, room is ready!
