Cottages Cleaning SOP

✓ 0/25 tasks ⏰ Full day job
🔑 Key Access: Keybox code is 108. Equipment stored under stairs. Leave cottage key inside on table when finished.
🍳 Step 1
Kitchen deep clean
▼
Clear all surfaces and counters completely
Wash all dishes, pots, pans, and utensils thoroughly
Wipe down all countertops with cleaning spray
Clean stovetop/hob (remove burner grates, clean spills)
Wipe down fridge exterior and handles
Check fridge interior, remove old food, wipe shelves if dirty
Empty kitchen bin, replace with new bag
Sweep and mop kitchen floor
🧼 Supply check: Refill dish soap, hand soap, sponges, and bin bags if needed. Supplies stored under stairs.
🛏️ Step 2
Bedrooms & living areas
▼
Strip all beds (remove sheets, pillowcases, duvet covers)
Put dirty linens in laundry basket to take to main house
Make all beds with fresh linens from storage cupboard
Dust all bedroom surfaces (nightstands, dressers, windowsills)
Hoover all bedroom floors thoroughly
Dust living room surfaces (tables, shelves, TV stand)
Hoover living room carpet/floor and under furniture
Empty all bins in bedrooms and living areas
🚿 Step 3
Bathrooms complete
▼
Clean toilet bowl with toilet cleaner and brush
Wipe down toilet exterior, seat, and base
Scrub shower/bathtub including walls and floor
Clean sink and countertop thoroughly
Clean mirror with glass cleaner
Refill hand soap, body wash, shampoo if needed
Replace toilet roll if less than half remaining
Place fresh towels (2 large, 2 small per bedroom)
Sweep and mop bathroom floor
🧺 Towel protocol: Each bedroom gets 2 large bath towels + 2 small hand towels. Check cupboard inventory before starting.
✅ Step 4
Final checks & lockup
▼
Check all windows are closed and locked
Turn off all lights in every room
Turn heating to appropriate setting (check weather)
Check all appliances are off (oven, stove, etc.)
Do final walkthrough: all rooms clean, beds made, bathrooms ready
Leave cottage key on kitchen table inside
Lock front door using keybox
Return all cleaning equipment to storage under stairs
🏡 Quality standard: Cottage should look completely untouched, as if no one has stayed. Every surface clean, all linens fresh, nothing out of place.
