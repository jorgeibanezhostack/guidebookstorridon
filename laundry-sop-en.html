Laundry SOP

✓ 0/17 tasks ⏱️ All-day job
⏰ Timing note: Laundry is an all-day job with lots of machine waiting time. You can start slightly later than usual and use waiting periods for other tasks or breaks. Especially important if doing rooms + laundry on the same day.
📖 Physical guide available: The complete Laundry Guide is in the Laundry Room as a physical copy. Refer to it for detailed machine instructions and troubleshooting.
👕 Step 1
Sorting & preparation
▼
Collect all dirty linens from rooms (if doing room changeovers)
Collect staff linen from staff laundry basket
Sort laundry into piles: whites, colors, towels, delicates
Check all pockets and remove any items
Treat any visible stains before washing
Sorting categories
  • Whites: Sheets, pillowcases, white towels
  • Colors: Colored linens, duvet covers
  • Towels: Bath towels, hand towels (separate if very dirty)
  • Delicates: Items requiring gentle cycle
🔄 Step 2
Washing cycles
▼
Load first batch into washing machine (don't overload)
Add appropriate detergent (follow machine/guide instructions)
Select correct wash program and temperature
Start washing cycle
Set timer/reminder for when cycle finishes
Repeat for remaining sorted piles throughout the day
⏳ Use waiting time wisely: While machines run, you can clean the laundry room, organize shelves, or work on other assigned tasks. Check machines periodically to avoid delays.
☀️ Step 3
Drying & folding
▼
Transfer washed items to dryer or hang to air dry (check labels)
Select appropriate dryer setting if using machine
Remove items promptly when dry to prevent wrinkles
Fold or hang all dried items neatly
Sort folded items: guest linens vs staff linens
📦 Step 4
Organization & storage
▼
Return guest linens to upstairs cupboards (don't let them pile up!)
Place staff linens on staff shelf in Laundry Room
Organize shelves and work surfaces in Laundry Room
Clean washing machine and dryer (lint trap, door seals)
Wipe down all surfaces and folding table
Check detergent and supplies levels, report if low
Sweep/mop Laundry Room floor
Storage locations reminder
  • Guest linens: Upstairs cupboard (up brown stairs, first right)
  • Guest towels: Big wardrobe upstairs (opposite linen cupboard)
  • Staff linens/towels: Staff shelf in Laundry Room
  • Bath mats (staff): Shelf next to big table in Laundry Room
  • Cleaning supplies: Cleaning storage (first door right in Laundry Room)
⚠️ Critical rule: ALWAYS take fresh linen and towels upstairs to refill the cupboards. Don't let clean items pile up in the Laundry Room!
