Cottages Cleaning SOP

✓ 0/25 tasks 🏡 Full property clean
🔑 Key access: Cottages often use the 108 key box code. Double-check code before departure and return keys immediately after finishing.
📍 Equipment location: Hoovers and mops are under the stairs in each cottage. In Stables, find them in the first room when you enter.
🍽️ Step 1
Kitchen deep clean
▼
Empty and clean fridge thoroughly
Wipe down all counters and surfaces
Clean stove top and oven (inside and outside)
Clean sink and taps until shiny
Wash all dishes, pots, pans and utensils
Empty bins and replace with fresh liners
Refill dish soap and hand soap
Replace sponges and tea towels with fresh ones
Sweep and mop kitchen floor
Kitchen supplies to check & refill
  • Dish soap and hand soap
  • Clean sponges and scourers
  • Fresh tea towels (at least 2)
  • Bin liners
  • Paper towels (if provided)
🛏️ Step 2
Bedrooms & linens
▼
Strip all beds of used linens
Make all beds with fresh sheets and pillowcases
Place fresh towels in each bedroom (per cottage checklist)
Dust all furniture surfaces in bedrooms
Hoover all bedroom floors thoroughly
Empty bedroom bins if present
🛏️ Linen quantities vary by cottage: Check the specific cottage checklist for exact towel and bedding requirements. Each cottage has different capacities.
🚿 Step 3
Bathrooms
▼
Clean toilet thoroughly (bowl, seat, base, behind)
Scrub shower/bathtub and remove soap scum
Clean sink, taps and counters
Clean mirrors and any glass surfaces
Refill toilet paper (full roll)
Refill hand soap and any toiletries
Place fresh bath mat on floor
Empty bathroom bin and replace liner
Sweep and mop bathroom floor
🏠 Step 4
Living areas & final touches
▼
Dust all surfaces in living room (tables, shelves, TV stand)
Hoover all living area floors and rugs
Arrange cushions and throw blankets neatly
Check windows for smudges, clean if needed
Final walkthrough: check all sinks, bins, surfaces
Open windows briefly to air out cottage
Lock all doors and return keys to key box/office
⚠️ Cottage-specific quirks: Each cottage may have unique features or issues. Read the cottage-specific notes in the full cleaning guide before starting. Ask if unsure!
✅ Final inspection checklist: Kitchen clean? Beds made? Bathrooms spotless? Floors hoovered? Bins empty? Supplies refilled? If yes to all, cottage is guest-ready!
