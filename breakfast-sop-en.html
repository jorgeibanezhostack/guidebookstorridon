Breakfast Service SOP

✓ 0/22 tasks 👥 Up to 10 guests
🕐 7:00 - 8:00 AM
Preparation phase
▼
Be in kitchen at 7:00 AM
Prepare 10 small clay plates for tomato + mozzarella starter
Cut 3 cherry tomatoes in half per plate
Add 2 mozzarella slices per plate
Prepare 10 deeper clay plates for egg dishes
📸 View setup photos in Google Drive
Egg dish assembly (per plate)
  • 5 drops olive oil at bottom
  • Layer of baby spinach from guest fridge
  • 4 cubes of feta cheese on top
  • 1 tablespoon chorizo spread around
  • Pour beaten eggs (22-24 eggs total in jug for 10 plates)
  • Sprinkle Italian seasoning to cover surface
🛒 Products used: Chef's Larder Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Nature's Pick Baby Spinach, Greek Feta Cheese (Original), Cowbelle Semi-Skimmed Milk
Assemble all 10 egg dishes following recipe
Prepare 2 large French presses (6 spoons coffee each)
Boil water using kettle and fill presses
Dietary adaptations: Vegetarian = remove chorizo. Vegan = notify at check-in that breakfast is not adapted.
🕐 8:00 - 9:00 AM
Guest service
▼
Listen for guests arriving in dining area
Greet guests and ask if they want coffee
Bring French press to table if yes
Point out sugar, cream, and tea at hot water station
Microwave cooking process
  • Standard batch (4 plates): 5 min max power → stir → 2 min more
  • Quick batch (2 plates): 3 min max power → stir → 1 min more
  • Eggs should puff up slightly when ready
  • Repeat process for remaining plates
💡 Use 4-plate batches during peak service (8:15-8:45 AM). Use 2-plate batches for staggered arrivals or late guests.
Cook egg plates in batches (4 or 2 depending on guest flow)
Prepare white plates with starter + 1 bolillo + 1 sweet roll (or 2 toast slices)
Add cooked egg plate to each white plate
Deliver complete plates to guests
📸 View cooking & plating photos in Google Drive
🕐 9:00 - 10:00 AM
Kitchen recovery
▼
Collect used plates with wooden tray
Transfer leftovers (milk, juice, coffee, unused prep) to volunteer kitchen
Bring all dishes to B&B kitchen
Turn on hot water tap and plug drain
Fill sink with hot water and dish soap
Wash all plates and utensils
Timing goal: Complete dish washing by 10:00 AM so kitchen is free for volunteer shift start.
🕐 After 10:00 AM
Handover to volunteers
▼
Empty trash bins
Wipe down all surfaces and tables
Sweep/mop floor
Confirm kitchen is ready for volunteer use
ℹ️ Leave kitchen in clean state for volunteer shift.
