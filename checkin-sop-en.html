Guest Check-In SOP

✓ 0/18 tasks 🏠 Torridon Estate
📋 Note: Check-in is primarily performed by managers/owners (Felix, Sara, Team Leader). You will only be assigned this task during high season or when supporting the main reception desk.
🔔 Step 1
Guest arrival & greeting
▼
How guests notify arrival
  • Doorbell ring
  • Phone call to reception number
  • WhatsApp message to reception phone
Open WhatsApp and locate the pinned message with today's check-in list
Greet guest at entrance: "Hello! Welcome to Torridon Estate. I'm [your name]"
Ask for their name and reservation details
Check WhatsApp pinned list to find which room they're assigned to
🍳 Step 2
Breakfast information
▼
Season-dependent service: Breakfast location changes based on season (high/low).
High Season (Summer/Warm Months)
☀️ Dining Room Service
  • Time: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Location: Dining Room (point to right door from entrance)
  • Menu: Eggs with chorizo, spinach, feta cheese
  • Available: Coffee, granola, cereals
Low Season (Cold Months)
❄️ In-Room Service
  • Breakfast is delivered directly to guest rooms
  • Inform guest of approximate delivery time
Inform: "Breakfast is served from 8:00 to 9:00 AM in the Dining Room" (point to right door)
Mention menu: eggs with chorizo, spinach, feta, plus coffee, granola, cereals
Ask about dietary restrictions: "Do you have any dietary restrictions? Dairy, meat, vegetarian?"
Note restrictions to communicate to breakfast staff
🏛️ Step 3
Property tour & room guidance
▼
Walk guest toward their room while sharing property history
Explain: "This is a 150-year-old house with historical significance"
Mention: "Different families have lived here over the years"
When reaching first floor, explain layout clearly
Privacy reminder: Right side of the property is the family's private area. Guests must stay in the left section where the 5 B&B rooms are located.
Point right: "The right side is the family's private residence, please do not enter"
Guide left: "All five guest rooms are on this left section of the property"
Walk guest to their assigned room
🔑 Step 4
Key handover & checkout info
▼
Hand room key to guest
Explain: "Here is your room key"
Inform checkout time: "Check-out is between 10:00 and 10:30 AM"
Request: "Please leave the key on the door when you check out"
Point to information boards showing WiFi details and local recommendations
Show contact numbers: "Manager and host numbers are on the board for any questions"
Encourage messaging: "Please send a message for the fastest response"
📝 Quick reference: Breakfast 8-9 AM | Check-out 10-10:30 AM | WiFi & contacts on wall boards
Say farewell: "Enjoy your stay! Let us know if you need anything"
Return to reception and mark check-in as complete in WhatsApp group or app
